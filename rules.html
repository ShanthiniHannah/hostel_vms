Rules & Guidelines

Please read and follow the visitor policies carefully to ensure safety and order in the hostel premises.

  1. All visitors must register at the hostel entrance before entry.
  2. Visitors must carry a valid government-issued photo ID.
  3. Visitor entry is allowed only during visiting hours (9 AM ‚Äì 6 PM).
  4. Students are responsible for their visitors' conduct.
  5. No overnight stay is allowed for visitors without prior permission.
  6. Visitors must wear the visitor badge provided at the time of entry.
  7. Warden approval is required for special visitor cases.
  8. Smoking, alcohol, or any form of substance abuse is strictly prohibited.
  9. Visitors must leave the hostel premises before the closing time.
  10. Management reserves the right to deny entry or remove any visitor violating the rules.
